I am a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science at Wake Forest University.

I completed my B.S. in Data Science at Peking University in 2020 and will receive my M.S. degree in Computer Science at Wake Forest University in 2023. At Wake, I have since been working as a research assistant for Dr. Minghan Chen for more than one academic year in person. During my time in her lab, my work has focused on deep learning approaches and parameter optimization algorithms for bio-related applications. Some academic achievements have been achieved and published at present. Before working with Dr. Minghan Chen, I completed some research topics and my undergraduate thesis project supervised by Dr. Xuanzhe Liu and Dr. Gang Huang at Peking University, mainly focusing on the computing performance and applications of mobile devices with the techniques of deep learning.

After living in North Carolina for more than one year, I have become familiar with and love the pace of living and research work in the United States. I hope to continue my studies and research on a better platform as a Ph.D. student.

For more about my academic work and research experience, please refer to my CV.
