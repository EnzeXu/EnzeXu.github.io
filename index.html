徐恩泽



I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the College of William & Mary, advised by Dr. Yifan Sun in the areas of GPU architecture and performance modeling. My Chinese name is 徐恩泽. You can pronounce my name as "En-zeh Shoo".

I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science from Wake Forest University in 2023, and my B.S. in Data Science and Bigdata Technology from Peking University in 2020. During my time at Wake Forest, I served as a research assistant to Dr. Minghan Chen, focusing on deep learning for biologically-related applications, resulting in several published academic achievements. Prior to this, my undergraduate research, supervised by Dr. Xuanzhe Liu and Dr. Gang Huang, explored computing performance and applications in mobile devices through deep learning techniques.

For more information about my academic work and research experience, please see my CV (updated in Mar. 2024).
